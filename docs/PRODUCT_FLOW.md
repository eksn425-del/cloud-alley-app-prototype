# Product Flow

## Product Goal

Cloud Alley App Prototype translates a residential design concept into a mobile-first service experience for a low-altitude smart community. It connects architectural decisions with resident-facing functions such as drone delivery, balcony environment monitoring, community notices, shared spaces, and low-carbon lifestyle feedback.

## Core Loop

```text
residential masterplan + facade / balcony strategy + low-altitude delivery scenario
-> AI-assisted spatial trade-off discussion
-> app service logic
-> resident interaction prototype
-> design feedback for planning and facade strategy
```

## Input Layer

The design logic considers:

- site masterplan
- building spacing
- drone route corridor
- balcony projection depth
- takeoff and landing points
- wind and facade exposure
- resident service needs
- community shared-space strategy

## AI-assisted Reasoning Layer

AI supports concept-level decision making by comparing:

- delivery route efficiency
- route safety and clearance
- balcony privacy and projection risk
- facade wind pressure and planting comfort
- relationship between low-altitude logistics and pedestrian life
- how app services should reflect spatial decisions

## App Output Layer

The public prototype expresses these decisions through:

- delivery tracking
- receiving preference
- balcony facade status
- plant and irrigation state
- community notices
- shared-space reservation
- low-carbon statistics

## Feedback Layer

The app prototype can feed back into design discussion:

- if drone delivery dominates the experience, landing points may need redesign
- if balcony projection creates route conflict, facade strategy may need adjustment
- if resident services are too scattered, community node planning may need refinement
- if environmental indicators are unclear, sensor and UI logic should be simplified

## Product Claim

This is not a full AI product yet. It is a low-altitude community app prototype with an AI-assisted spatial decision workflow behind it.
